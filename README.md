# 👨‍💻 About Me

Hi 👋 I'm **Akshay**, a B.Tech student passionate about merging **web engineering** with **AI innovation**.  
I thrive on building systems that are not just functional, but **scalable**, **intelligent**, and designed with clarity.


Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in the **MERN stack** and modern frontend frameworks, learning how to craft responsive interfaces and robust backend systems. My journey into **Machine Learning** and **Deep Learning** opened new doors — from experimenting with neural networks to deploying models that solve real‑world problems. Today, I’m equally comfortable designing a polished UI as I am fine‑tuning a transformer model.


What excites me most is the intersection of **Generative AI** and **Agentic AI**. I enjoy exploring how large language models, RAG pipelines, and agentic workflows can be integrated into production‑ready applications. For me, development isn’t just about writing code — it’s about engineering solutions that combine **clean architecture**, **scalable design**, and **intelligent systems** to make technology more impactful and accessible.
